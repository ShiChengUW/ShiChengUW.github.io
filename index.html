Oct-10-2023, Surgery Constructions for 3d Theories, Part I: Matter Circles and Links, arXiv:2310.XXXX

This project is difficult to me, and I often feel having no tool to address the problem. Although the computation is little, but the amout of thinking is a lot. Finally, it looks that the result opens a door to a colorful garden, in which I can see some familar trees and flowers (MTC, category, higher forms symmetries) are already there waiting to me. Some parts of working notes are aviliable here (II).

Aug-02-2023, 3d N=2 theories and plumbing graphs: adding matter, gauging, and new dualities, arXiv:2302.13371 was accepted by JHEP.
This paper studies the dualities of abelian theories. We proposed a new quiver diagram --- plumbing graphs with matter nodes. We notice that the bifundamental matter is a bit subtle. After a few weeks of trying to break the barrier of understanding and to derive superpotential triangles, everything is smoothly and quickly done. The brane web is also a bit subtle. After this work, I can clearly see that there should be a closed relation between 3d theories and operations on three-manifolds. Some parts of working notes (I, II) are attached to present the working style.
